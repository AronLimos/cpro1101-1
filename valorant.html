What is Valorant?

Valorant is a 5v5 multiplayer first-person shooter (FPS) where one team attacks and the other defends. The main game mode, Search and Destroy, is very similar to CS:GO. The attacking team's goal is to plant a bomb (called a spike) and have it detonate, while the defending team tries to avoid the bomb by defusing it in condition with the timer. Regardless of whether the spike is planted or not, if a squad is wiped out before any other victory condition is met, the opposing squad will win.

Let's Play!

Valorant has four main permanent PvP game modes. Unrated, Competitive, Spike Rush, and Deathmatch. Unrated(Search and Destroy), Competitive(Search and Destroy with rank values), Spike Rush(Fast Pace Search and Destroy), and Deathmatch(Team vs Team). Let's play with the most popular gamemode, Unrated. Once loaded in, A map will pop open, the system will choose only one map to play. After that, character selction time. You will be able to choose one character per person. Choose certain characters in order to win as a team First to 13 wins! Too scared? Alternatively, you can practice by yourself in the Range a practice map that provides unlimited ammo, abilities, and unlocked Agents to hone your skills against bots.
